Design system

Drawer

An edge-anchored panel that slides in over the page for tasks that complement the current view instead of replacing it — record detail, editing, filters. The most-used overlay in ATTRUS. For a blocking decision use a Modal instead.

03Sticky glass header & footer

The header and footer are always fixed and use a liquid-glass background — translucent --glass-white with a --glass-blur backdrop filter. As the body scrolls, content passes beneath them with a frosted blur, keeping the title and actions reachable at all times. Scroll the panel below to see it.

Frosted chrome .drawer-header · .drawer-footer

Header is position: sticky; top: 0; footer is position: sticky; bottom: 0. Both carry the glass background + blur. A solid fallback applies where backdrop-filter is unsupported.

Accessibility & rules

ConcernRequirement
Close buttonAlways an icon-button with aria-label="Close"; never icon-only without a label.
Focus orderHeader → body → footer. Initial focus lands on the first interactive element (or the close).
Title associationaria-labelledby on the drawer points at the title id.
Sticky contrastGlass uses --glass-white 58%; title/actions keep AA as content scrolls beneath.
Ink heroInverse foreground tokens for AA on the dark surface; close button uses an overlay-white fill.
Footer actionsPrimary is the affirmative; destructive uses .btn-danger + a cancel path.

One primary per footerLead with a single affirmative action; demote the rest to ghost/secondary.

Don't stack search + tabs + metadataPick one header enhancement — piling them on buries the title.

Put totals on the leftUse space-between so the amount reads before the commit action.

Don't exceed 3 footer actionsMore than three competes with the primary. Move extras into the header overflow.

05Sizes

Four widths for right/left drawers. Bottom drawers size to content up to 80vh. Never exceed 720px — past that, use a full route, not a drawer.

06Dismissal

Drawers are non-blocking, so they dismiss freely — there is no "locked" drawer. If a drawer holds unsaved edits, prompt before discarding.

Cancel / footer secondaryCloses

Swipe down (bottom drawer)Closes on threshold velocity
Unsaved changesPrompt before closing — never silently discard

Settlement & transfers

Settlement
How long does settlement take?
Local rails clear same-day; SWIFT typically 1–2 business days depending on clearing-house cutoffs.
Which currencies are supported?
BRL, USD, EUR, MXN, COP and CLP, with more LATAM rails rolling out.
Transfers
What is the FX spread?
The difference between the mid-market rate and your quoted rate. It's 0% on inter-company transfers.
Can I cancel a pending transfer?
Yes, while a transfer is Pending. Once Settled it can only be reversed via a new transaction.

09Don'ts

Left drawer on desktopThe desktop sidebar is already persistent — a left drawer duplicates navigation. Reserve .drawer-left for mobile.

Bottom drawer on desktopBottom sheets are a thumb pattern. On desktop use a right drawer or a popover.

Drawer wider than 720pxIt stops being a side panel and starts hiding the context it's meant to preserve. Use a full route.

Blocking decisions in a drawerDestructive or system-blocking choices belong in a Modal — drawers are dismissable by design.

Solid, opaque header that hides scrollKeep the frosted glass chrome — it signals there's more content beneath and keeps actions reachable.
