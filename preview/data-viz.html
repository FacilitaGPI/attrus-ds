01Categorical

Eight qualitative series for distinct, unordered categories. Anchored on Royal and Sky, then extended with cool, restrained hues — ordered so neighbours stay distinguishable. Use in legend order; never more than eight.

02Sequential & diverging

Ordered scales for encoding magnitude. Sequential is a single Sky hue, low→high. Diverging runs danger → neutral → success around a meaningful midpoint — use it for variance, deltas, or anything centred on zero.

04Structure

The non-data parts of a chart — gridlines, axes, tracks, reference lines — all resolve to semantic tokens, so they recede correctly in both themes without per-chart tuning.
