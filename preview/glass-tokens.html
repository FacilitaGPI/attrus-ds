05Liquid glass

Translucent layers used for floating chrome — toolbars over imagery, modals over a dimmed canvas, navigation that overlaps content. Every glass surface combines a translucent fill, a backdrop-filter blur, and a hairline border.

--glass-white 58% white — default light glass over mid-bright bg
--glass-white-hi 78% white — opaque-feeling, max readability
--glass-white-lo 38% white — subtle frosted hint
--glass-tint Bluish-white tint — system flavor
--glass-ink 55% navy — default dark glass
--glass-ink-hi 78% navy — opaque dark
--glass-ink-lo 35% navy — soft hint of dark
