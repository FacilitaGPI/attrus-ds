02Color · raw palette

Eight families anchored in the official brand book. Three brand scales (Navy primary, Royal secondary, Sky accent), thirteen neutrals, four feedback tones, and the white-alpha overlay set. All numbered 50 → 900 on a perceptually consistent OKLCH ramp.

Navy & Black dominate high-impact pieces (covers, OOH, institutional social). White creates pause and reading rhythm. Royal appears in pieces with more energy than Navy. Sky always as highlight or action — never as a dominant background.

Semantic feedback — success, warning, danger, info

Subdued, sober tones — never bright. Each family is a 10-stop scale that pairs predictably: 50 for backgrounds, 100 for borders, 500 for solids, 700 for foreground text on a 50 bg. The full scale supports dark-mode inversions — e.g. 300 text on 800 bg.
