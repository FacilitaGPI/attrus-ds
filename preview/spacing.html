07Spacing

A 4px base scale — dense enough for data tables, generous enough for marketing layouts. Every padding, margin, and gap in the system snaps to one of the canonical steps. The integer rungs carry the rhythm; three half-steps (--space-2-5, --space-3-5, --space-4-5) exist for dense control padding where a full step is too much. There is no 11px, no 23px; pick the nearest stop or, if the scale is genuinely lacking, propose a new token.
