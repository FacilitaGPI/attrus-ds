Line height & letter spacing

--lh-tight (1.15)
Used for display + H1 — tight vertical rhythm so multi-line headlines hold together as one shape.
--lh-snug (1.3)
H2–H5 — comfortable but compact, lets adjacent headings sit close to their content without crowding.
--lh-normal (1.5)
Body default — the standard for any continuous prose under 16px. Optimised for screen reading without feeling airy.
--lh-relaxed (1.65)
Body LG and lead paragraphs — gives marketing copy and section intros space to breathe across longer line lengths.
