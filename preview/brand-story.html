01Positioning

Who ATTRUS is — and just as important, who ATTRUS is not. The negation is what makes the positioning sharp.

Who is ATTRUS

ATTRUS is the financial infrastructure that unifies the flow of international transactions between businesses.

We are the layer that connects markets, currencies and local rails — with the security of a regulated institution and the speed of a modern platform.

We are not A consumer fintech. We don't serve individuals shopping for a card.
We are not A bank. We don't custody, we don't lend, we don't compete with our clients' banking relationships.
We are The layer. The infrastructure. The platform that lets businesses move money across borders without rebuilding it themselves.

03History

Eight years of building infrastructure for cross-border payments in Latin America. Each milestone is a deliberate step, not an accident.

2017
The beginning
Three partners found the company to revolutionise payments in Brazil — closing the gap between developed economies and emerging markets.
2018–19
Integrations
Clients in more than 10 countries. USD 2 billion in total processed volume.
2020
Feature expansion
Expanded beyond payments — integrated fraud prevention as a native part of the platform.
2021–22
Local operations
Reached Colombia and Chile with on-the-ground teams. 350% growth in 2022.
2023
Regulatory milestone
First regulated Payment Institution for FX in Brazil — a category-defining licence from the central bank.
2024
Exponential growth
+220% in TPV, +37% in active client base, +260% in revenue.
2025–now
Sustained growth
H1 2025 surpasses the full year of 2024. Argentina operations launching. Platform expanding into structured account opening and corporate treasury.

04Manifesto

The diagnosis, the response, the commitment. Read aloud, the manifesto is what ATTRUS would say if it had to introduce itself in 60 seconds.

The economy is global, but the path of money is still fragmented. Each country, currency and regulation creates a different way of doing the same thing: paying and receiving.

We exist to simplify financial operations between countries, reducing the complexity that makes it hard for businesses to operate beyond their borders — offering clarity, structure and consistent support.

Where the world sees borders, we draw routes.

We face every challenge as a natural part of the evolution of our business and our clients. We believe proximity is a genuine differentiator and we uphold transparency as a principle. Our focus goes beyond technology: we work to remove barriers, expand borders and enable opportunities — so businesses can operate with safety, predictability and efficiency.

We are ATTRUS, the financial infrastructure that unifies the flow of international transactions between businesses. We deliver simplified transactions, controlled risk and structured international operations to sustain consistent growth.

05Mission · vision · goal · philosophy

Four short statements that organise long-term direction. Together they say what we exist to do, where we aim, how we measure ourselves, and how we operate.

01 · Mission

What we exist to do

To revolutionise the way businesses handle the global payments market — making cross-border financial operations as routine as domestic ones.

02 · Vision

Where we aim

Our unwavering focus on client satisfaction and success drives us to deliver innovative, technologically rigorous payment solutions.

03 · Goal

How we measure ourselves

Sustainable growth. Growing is essential, but growing sustainably — without breaking quality, compliance or team — is what defines us.

04 · Philosophy

How we operate

Global Perspective, Local Action. Being global is not just a goal, it's our way of operating — adapting strategy to each local market.

06Cultural values

Four cultural pillars. Used in hiring rubrics, performance reviews, and the way we describe how we work — externally and internally.

01
Commitment to results

Personal and team responsibility for delivery. Each person owns their commitment to the company's outcomes; results are everyone's job, not just leadership's.

02
Collaboration

Complementarity. Each person plays a unique, complementary role — and the company is incomplete without any of them. Alignment and integration of talent over individual heroism.

03
Continuous development

Constant learning and improvement. The market changes; the regulation changes; the team must change with them. Growth and development are baked into how we operate.

04
Excellence

High performance. Quality. We hold ourselves to the standards of the institutions we operate alongside — regulators, central banks, corporate treasuries. The bar is set externally, not internally.
