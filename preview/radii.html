Layout

08Radii

Mostly soft, never sharp. Cards and inputs sit at --radius-md; containers grow to --radius-lg; hero panels reach --radius-xl. Three in-between rungs (--radius-sm-md 8px, --radius-md-lg 12px, --radius-lg-xl 16px) fill the gaps for tiles and medium cards. Buttons are unique — fully rounded with --radius-pill regardless of size.

Usage rule — slightly rounded everywhere, fully rounded for buttons. Cards & tables md · Inputs & chips sm · Modals & drawers md · Receipts & hero md–lg · Icon tiles sm–md. All buttons & pills use --radius-pill regardless of size — the clearest brand tell.
